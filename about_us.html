About us

NHCE, or New Horizon College of Engineering, is an autonomous engineering college in Bangalore, India, affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University and accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade. Located in Bangalore's IT corridor, it is known for its strong industry-academia interface, excellent infrastructure, and high placement rates. The college offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs and emphasizes both technical education and overall personality development.
